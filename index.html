🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
Open the app → Tap your profile → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
The game is running slowly or crashing
Try closing and restarting the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. For older devices, try using smaller board sizes.
How do I play online with friends?
Use "Network Game" from the main menu. One player hosts and shares a QR code, others scan it to join. Both devices must be on the same WiFi network.
What's included with Vertex Pro?
Pro version removes all advertisements and provides unlimited gameplay. Future updates will include premium themes and advanced AI opponents.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to Settings → iTunes & App Store → Apple ID → Purchase History, find your purchase, and select "Report a Problem".

Tips & Strategy

🎯 Gameplay Strategy

  • Think Ahead: Avoid giving your opponent easy boxes to complete
  • Chain Reactions: Try to set up multiple boxes you can complete in one turn
  • Sacrifice Strategy: Sometimes giving away a few boxes early prevents bigger losses later

🤖 AI Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Random moves - great for beginners
  • Medium: Avoids obvious mistakes and completes available boxes
  • Hard: Advanced strategy with look-ahead planning

📐 Board Sizes

  • Small (3×3): Quick games, perfect for learning
  • Medium (5×5): Balanced gameplay with more strategy
  • Large (7×7): Long games with complex strategies
