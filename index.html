üôã Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?
iOS: Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Android: Main menu ‚Üí Pro version ‚Üí Restore. Make sure you're signed into the same account used for the original purchase.
The game is running slowly or crashing
Try closing and restarting the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. For older devices, try using smaller board sizes. On Android, clear the app cache in Settings.
How do I play online with friends?
Use "Network Game" from the main menu. One player hosts and shares a QR code, others scan it to join. Both devices must be on the same WiFi network. Camera permission is required for QR scanning.
What's included with Vertex Pro?
Pro version removes all advertisements and provides unlimited gameplay. Future updates will include premium themes and advanced AI opponents.
How do I join the Android beta test?
Click the "Join Android Beta" button above, then click "Become a tester" on Google Play. After joining, search for "Vertex: Dotgrid" in the Play Store to download the beta version.
Are AI difficulty levels available?
Currently, the AI plays at a single balanced difficulty level. Multiple difficulty options (Easy, Medium, Hard) will be added in a future update based on player feedback.
Haptic feedback not working (Android)
Check that vibration is enabled in your device settings and that the haptic feedback toggle is on in the game settings. Some devices may have limited vibration support.
Can I get a refund?
iOS: Contact Apple Support or visit reportaproblem.apple.com. Android: Visit Google Play Store ‚Üí Your purchases ‚Üí Find the app ‚Üí Request refund.

Privacy Policy

Simple Promise: Vertex respects your privacy. We collect minimal information and store most data locally on your device.

Effective Date: January 15, 2025 | Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Player Name: Optional display name for multiplayer games (stored locally)
  • Game Settings: Preferences such as sound, haptic feedback, and visual settings (stored locally)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Game Progress: Local game statistics, scores, and achievements (stored locally)
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage patterns through Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Crash Reports: Automatic crash reporting to help improve app stability

Data Storage and Security

  • iOS: Data stored using iOS Keychain and UserDefaults
  • Android: Data stored using Android SharedPreferences and internal storage
  • Cross-platform: Game data stored using Hive (local database)
  • Network Multiplayer: Direct device-to-device communication, no external servers

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Collects basic usage analytics (both platforms)
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements in the free version (both platforms)
  • In-App Purchases: Processed through Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)

Platform-Specific Privacy Features

iOS:

  • App Tracking Transparency: We request permission before tracking for advertising
  • Camera: Only used for QR code scanning (with permission)
  • Privacy Controls: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising

Android:

  • Required Permissions: INTERNET (analytics/ads), VIBRATE (haptic feedback), CAMERA (QR scanning), ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE (multiplayer)
  • Privacy Dashboard: View and control app permissions in Android settings
  • Advertising Controls: Google Settings > Ads > Reset advertising ID

Children's Privacy

Vertex: Dotgrid is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We:

  • Do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Use age-appropriate ad content settings
  • Comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Your Privacy Rights

  • View Data: All data is stored locally and accessible through game settings
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Advertising Controls: Purchase Pro version to remove all advertisements
  • Analytics Opt-Out: iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security; Android: Google Settings > Usage & Diagnostics

App Store Compliance
